SCHOOL PROFILE

Kirungu Primary School is a public primary school
in Kamacharia location.
The school is managed/sponsored by central government.
The institution offers Kenya Certifiacate Of Primary Education(KCPE)
which is controlled by the Kenya National Examination Council(KNEC)under
Ministry of Education.
