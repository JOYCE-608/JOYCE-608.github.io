Choosing the right school for a child is one of the most important decisions any parent or guardian
will make, and choosing a curriculum that opens the world for the child gives them an added
advantage. Admission to the various international schools in Kenya comes with various
requirements. To be admitted to Brookhurst International School you must have attained the
following:

IGCSE – Admission from Year 1 (5+ years old) l to Year 10 (15+ years old)

A LEVEL– Must have at least scored the following grades in IGCSE and its equivalent (English
language grade C, Mathematics grade C and a minimum of grade C in subjects one desire to study

THE APPLICATION PROCESS

1) Download, fill in and submit the application form.
2)The school will contact you and schedule for a visit.
3)Visit the school for interview or schedule arrange for a Skype Interview (for non-residents) .

If you would like your child to attend Brookhurst international school, please feel free to contact our
offices at Branton court off Ndemi lane Ngong road in Nairobi or you are welcome to arrange a visit
at the school in Kiserian, pipeline road.

You can also send us an email to info@bookhurstschool.or.ke Application forms are also available at
our offices and should be filled and returned to the office.
